What are Marcus NFT ?
Marcus...

Marcus is a sulky pineapple, he gets angry for nothing and that all day long. No matter what accessories he wears, it doesn't help his mood... 

On-Chain

The collection is entirely and exclusively available on the Polygon blockchain. We have chosen this chain so that you can get your own Marcus without paying several tens of dollars in gas fees. 
We will store all the 1010 NFTs directly on Polygon chain in order to have the best user experience. You'll be able to see all the Minted NFTs directly on the Marketplace.

Unique

The Marcus collection consists of 1010 pieces. Each Marcus is entirely unique thanks to a combination of more than 10 different handmade accessories. Rarity levels of the accessories have been carefully designed to make your Marcus stand out. To discover the accessories and other surprises, visit our Discord server!
